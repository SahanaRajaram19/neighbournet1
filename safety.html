Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines to ensure a secure experience on NeighbourNet.

Before Meeting

  • Verify Profiles: Check user ratings, reviews, and verification status
  • Communicate Clearly: Use our in-app messaging to discuss details before meeting
  • Share Minimal Information: Don't share personal contact details until you're comfortable
  • Trust Your Instincts: If something feels off, cancel and report the user

During Item Exchange

  • Meet in Public: Choose busy, well-lit public locations for exchanges
  • Bring a Friend: Consider bringing someone with you for added security
  • Inspect Items: Thoroughly check item condition before completing the transaction
  • Document Everything: Take photos of items and note any existing damage
  • Use Secure Payment: Complete all transactions through our platform

Online Safety

  • Protect Your Data: Never share passwords or financial information
  • Report Suspicious Activity: Use the report button for any concerning behavior
  • Be Cautious with Links: Don't click on suspicious links shared in messages
  • Verify Before Paying: Ensure you're dealing with verified users before making payments

Item Safety Guidelines

  • Handle with Care: Treat borrowed items as if they were your own
  • Report Damage: Immediately inform the owner of any damage or issues
  • Return on Time: Respect agreed-upon return dates and times
  • Clean Before Return: Return items in the same condition as received
  • Insurance Coverage: High-value items are covered by our insurance policy

Additional Safety Tips

Schedule Daytime Meetings

Plan exchanges during daylight hours when possible for better visibility and safety.

Share Your Location

Let a trusted friend or family member know where and when you're meeting someone.

Document Everything

Take photos of items, receipts, and keep records of all communications.

Check Reviews

Read user reviews and ratings before agreeing to any transaction.
